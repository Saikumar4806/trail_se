Admin Features

Manage Items

Manage subscription items, adjust prices, edit details, or remove products from the delivery network.

Administer all customers in the system. View roles, status, and perform customer management operations.

Manage Time Slots

Configure delivery time slots with capacity limits and day-wise availability settings.

Set and manage base subscription plan rates, frequencies, and pricing structures.

Calculate Salaries

Calculate and manage delivery partner salaries, bonuses, incentives, and payment status.

Track monthly subscription revenue, salary payouts, gross profit, payout ratio, and delivery economics.
